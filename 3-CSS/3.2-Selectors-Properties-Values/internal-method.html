Where It All Begins

Ai Hayasaka is one of the side characters from Kaguya Sama : Love Is War Series, which her role is to become Shinomiya Kaguya's Personal Maid. She study at the same school as Shinomiya Kaguya too.

Beautiful Essence

Ai is a beautiful young girl with blonde hair that is usually tied up at the left side of her head with a blue scrunchie and blue-eyes. She alters her appearance and choice of clothing to suit different roles and carry out her duties as Kaguya's maid.

Charmingly Inside and Outside

Despite her young age, Ai can quickly shift to different types of personalities to suit various kinds of situations and scenarios. She has four distinct personas which, aside from having a specific purpose, has its own unique background and upbringing.

Hayasaka’s New Chapter: Embracing Normalcy

In a bold move symbolizing her transition, Hayasaka has decided to leave her maid position behind. This significant change is marked by a dramatic transformation in her appearance—she has cut her hair, crafting a fresh new look for herself. This isn’t merely a change in style; it’s a powerful statement of her intent to embrace the life of a “normal” high school student. Hayasaka’s journey is not just about altering her hairstyle; it’s about reshaping her identity and stepping into a world where she can experience the quintessential moments of adolescence on her own terms.
